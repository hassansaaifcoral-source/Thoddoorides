Privacy Policy

Thoddoo Ride collects only what we need to rent you a bike safely and to keep our small fleet secure. This is a plain-language summary — please review and adjust it before going live.

What we collect

  • Your name and contact number (WhatsApp or phone)
  • Your booking details — bike, dates and payment method
  • A copy of your passport, and your driving licence if you provide one

How we use it

To confirm and manage your booking, verify your identity for the refundable deposit, and contact you about your rental. We do not sell your information or share it for marketing.

How long we keep it

Document copies are kept only for the duration of your rental and the deposit period, then deleted. Basic booking records may be retained for our accounts.

Your choices

You can ask us what we hold, or request deletion, at any time — just message us. Providing a passport copy is required to rent; the driving licence is optional.

Rental Agreement & Liability Waiver

This is a plain-language summary of the terms you accept when you rent an e-bike from Thoddoo Ride. The full signed version is provided at pickup. Please review and adapt it with a local legal advisor before going live.

1 · Who can ride

The rider is 16 years or older, in good health, and comfortable riding a bicycle. You agree to ride only within Thoddoo and to follow local rules and customs.

2 · The bike

We provide the e-bike in good working order with a helmet and lock. You'll check its condition with us at pickup and tell us about any existing damage before you ride.

3 · Safe use

You agree to wear the helmet, ride at safe speeds, not carry more people than the bike is built for, and never ride under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

4 · Care & return

Return the bike on time and in the same condition. Lock it whenever it's unattended. Late returns may be charged at the daily rate.

5 · Damage, loss & theft

You're responsible for damage, loss or theft during your rental. The refundable deposit may be applied toward repairs. Report any theft to us and the police immediately.

6 · Deposit

A refundable deposit is taken at pickup and returned in full when the bike comes back undamaged.

7 · Liability

You ride at your own risk. To the extent allowed by law, Thoddoo Ride is not liable for injury, loss or damage arising from your use of the bike, except where caused by our negligence or faulty equipment.

8 · Cancellation

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Within 24 hours, contact us and we'll do our best to help.
